About Me

I am a Full Stack Developer with a strong passion for learning and creating exceptional digital experiences. I specialize in both Front-End Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Angular) and Back-End Development (.NET Core Web API), alongside expertise in Database Management with SQL Server.

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Application and Business Administration (IT), supported by over 16+ years of experience in developing and managing software applications. From tackling algorithmic challenges to delivering scalable and user-friendly solutions, I am dedicated to achieving technical excellence.

Currently, I work at Lovely Group in India, where I channel my enthusiasm for technology into developing impactful applications. My career is driven by a strong foundation in communication, effective collaboration with clients and teams, and the ability to manage multiple priorities under pressure.

Beyond coding, I am a lifelong learner who enjoys exploring new technologies and refining my skills to stay ahead in a constantly evolving industry. I believe in giving my best to every project, no matter the challenge, and finding fulfillment in pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
